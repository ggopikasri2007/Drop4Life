Why Every Donor Counts

Blood donation isn’t just about giving — it’s about *connecting lives*. Each donor becomes part of a nationwide circle of care. With every pint, you’re giving hope, healing, and strength to someone in need.

  • Every 2 seconds, someone needs blood.
  • One donation can save up to 3 lives.
  • Donating regularly improves your heart health.
